# 06 — 書櫃空狀態插圖

**用途**：`/shelf` 沒有書時顯示
**輸出路徑**：`public/illustrations/empty-shelf.png`
**尺寸建議**：800 × 800（方形）
**搭配文案**：「還沒有書。加一本開始讀，或去探索找夥伴。」

## 概念

一個空的小書櫃只有最底層放著一兩本書、有一個空格擺著小盆栽，期待新書加入。

## Prompt

```
A soft watercolor illustration of a small empty bookshelf, viewed straight-on,
with three or four shelves. Most shelves are empty cream-colored wood,
but on the bottom shelf sit just two small books leaning slightly into each other,
and on a middle shelf a single tiny potted plant with small green leaves.
The composition feels gently expectant — there's room for more.
Soft warm light glows from one side, casting a long subtle shadow.

soft watercolor illustration, hand-painted texture, slightly grainy paper background,
warm parchment color palette (#F8F1E4 background, sage green #6B8064 and amber #D9B884
on the books and plant, walnut #3D2F1F for fine outlines),
literary cozy mood, square composition with the bookshelf occupying center,
plenty of cream parchment negative space around,
gentle bleeding edges, no harsh ink lines, no neon colors,
no figures, no text in image.
```

## Avoid

- 完全空的書櫃（會看起來像 broken）
- 太多裝飾（要保留「等待」的感覺）
