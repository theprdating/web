# 04 — Onboarding 第 4 頁：個人書房

**頁面文案**：「個人書房」+「想自己讀也很好。寫下進度與心得，留給未來的自己。」
**用途**：`/intro/4` 全螢幕背景
**輸出路徑**：`public/illustrations/intro-4.png`
**尺寸建議**：1080 × 1920（9:16）

## 概念

一個小人站在私人小書房裡，面前一張桌子、一盞溫暖檯燈、一本翻開的書、幾張便利貼貼在書邊。窗外可隱約看到夜色。傳達「自己也是好夥伴」的安靜獨處感。

## Prompt

```
A soft watercolor illustration: a single small hooded figure in a warm-toned beige robe (between sage and amber)
stands in a tiny intimate study, exactly in the style of the Folio logo (round head, two tiny dot eyes, no other features).
In front of the figure is a small wooden writing desk with a glowing brass-style table lamp,
an open book with a few hand-drawn sticky notes peeking out from the pages,
a tea cup and a fountain pen. Behind the desk a cozy bookshelf with rounded books in muted greens, creams,
and lavender. Through a small window, a quiet evening sky in soft lavender. Warm pool of light falls on the desk.

soft watercolor illustration, hand-painted texture, slightly grainy paper background,
warm parchment color palette (#F8F1E4 base, sage green #6B8064 and lavender #9B8FB5 as accents,
walnut #3D2F1F for fine outlines, amber #D9B884 for the lamp glow),
literary cozy mood, vertical 9:16 composition with bottom 30% empty cream space
for title text overlay, gentle bleeding edges,
no harsh ink lines, no neon colors, exactly one figure,
no text in image.
```

## Avoid

- 多人（這是「個人」頁）
- 現代家具（沙發、地毯太鮮豔）
- 食物特寫
