# 07 — 雙方 100% 慶祝插圖

**用途**：room 內兩人都讀完 100% 時跳出的慶祝 overlay 背景
**輸出路徑**：`public/illustrations/celebrate.png`
**尺寸建議**：800 × 1000（接近方形偏直）
**搭配文案**：「你們做到了」+ 雙方暱稱 +《書名》+ 共讀天數

## 概念

兩個 logo 風格的小人（沙鼠尾草綠 + 薰衣草紫）站在一本超大、向天空打開的書旁邊，書中升起一道柔和的光柱、撒出細小的紙片或星塵慶祝。要溫暖、不要派對風。

## Prompt

```
A soft watercolor illustration: two small hooded figures stand close together at the foot of a giant open book
that lies face-up on the ground. From the open pages, a gentle column of warm golden light rises into the sky,
carrying soft drifting paper-like petals, tiny stars, and curling page-fragments — a quiet celebration.
The figure on the left wears a sage green robe (#6B8064), the one on the right wears a lavender robe (#9B8FB5),
both in the exact style of the Folio logo (round heads, two tiny dot eyes, no other facial features).
Above them the sky is a tender cream-to-lavender gradient (echoing the Folio logo day-night split).
The mood is intimate and quiet — like a small private firework, not a loud festival.

soft watercolor illustration, hand-painted texture, slightly grainy paper background,
warm parchment color palette (#F8F1E4 base, sage green #6B8064 and lavender #9B8FB5 as the two characters,
amber #D9B884 for the rising light, walnut #3D2F1F for fine outlines),
literary cozy mood, vertical-leaning composition with the upper 25% reserved as soft cream space
for headline text, gentle bleeding edges,
no harsh ink lines, no neon colors, exactly two figures,
no text in image.
```

## Avoid

- 鞭炮、煙火、彩帶（太派對）
- 強烈表情（人物只用兩點眼，不要笑臉）
- 多人聚會感
