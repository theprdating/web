# 03 — Onboarding 第 3 頁：共讀夥伴

**頁面文案**：「共讀夥伴」+「找到讀同一本書的人，從相同的字句開始一段故事。」
**用途**：`/intro/3` 全螢幕背景
**輸出路徑**：`public/illustrations/intro-3.png`
**尺寸建議**：1080 × 1920（9:16）

## 概念

兩個 logo 風格的小人——一個沙鼠尾草綠、一個薰衣草紫——並肩坐在月夜中的長凳上，共同捧著一本大書讀。書頁自然地延展成柔軟的雲海或光河。

## Prompt

```
A soft watercolor illustration: two small hooded figures sit side-by-side on a long curved bench
made from book pages — one figure in sage green robe (#6B8064) on the left, one in lavender robe (#9B8FB5)
on the right, exactly in the style of the Folio logo (round heads, two tiny dot eyes, no other facial features).
They share a single large open book held between their laps; their hooded silhouettes lean gently toward
each other as if quietly reading the same paragraph. Above them a peaceful evening sky transitions
from warm cream on the left to soft lavender twilight on the right (echoing the day-night split of the Folio logo),
with a tiny crescent moon and a few stars on the right side, soft floating clouds on the left.
The bench dissolves into pages of an even larger book at the bottom of the frame.

soft watercolor illustration, hand-painted texture, slightly grainy paper background,
warm parchment color palette (#F8F1E4 base, sage green #6B8064 and lavender #9B8FB5 as the two characters,
walnut #3D2F1F for fine outlines, amber #D9B884 for moon/star highlights only),
literary cozy mood, vertical 9:16 composition with bottom 30% reserved as empty cream space
for title text overlay, gentle bleeding edges,
no harsh ink lines, no neon colors, exactly two figures,
no text in image.
```

## Avoid

- 三個以上的人
- 強情緒（擁抱、親吻）— 要克制、平靜的陪伴感
- 電子裝置
