# 01 — Welcome 主視覺

**用途**：`/welcome` 頁全螢幕背景
**輸出路徑**：`public/illustrations/welcome.png`
**尺寸建議**：1080 × 1920（9:16 mobile-first）或 1242 × 2208
**已有版本**：是（你給的水彩 cafe 場景）；本 prompt 是想升級或重生時用

## Prompt

```
A soft watercolor illustration of a sunlit cozy reading nook —
a small wooden table holds an open book and a steaming cup of green tea,
beside it potted plants with gentle leaves, a tall bookshelf in the warm-lit
background filled with vintage books in muted greens, browns, and creams.
Morning light streams through a tall window, casting a gentle haze.
The mood is quiet, contemplative, and inviting — like the first moment
of a peaceful Sunday afternoon spent alone with a book.

soft watercolor illustration, hand-painted texture, slightly grainy paper background,
warm parchment color palette (#F8F1E4 background, sage green #6B8064 and lavender #9B8FB5 as accent tones,
walnut #3D2F1F for fine outlines, amber #D9B884 for sunlight highlights),
literary cozy mood, vertical 9:16 composition with empty cream space at top
(reserved for "歡迎來到 Folio" wordmark) and bottom (reserved for CTA button),
gentle bleeding edges, no harsh ink lines, no neon colors, no people in this version,
no text in image — leave clean composition for typography overlay.
```

## Avoid

- 任何文字（文字之後 by code 加上去）
- 強光斑、閃爍特效
- 現代電器、手機、電腦（純粹紙本書世界）
- 飽和橘黃（會與色票打架）

## 變體想法

- 黃昏版（`welcome-evening.png`）— 同場景但替換為黃昏暖光
- 雨天版（`welcome-rainy.png`）— 窗外下雨，更內向

## 備註

第一張你給的 AI 生成圖很好，這份 prompt 是讓你之後想換風或量產變體時用的，可不立即執行。
