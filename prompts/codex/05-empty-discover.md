# 05 — Discover 空狀態插圖

**用途**：`/discover` 頁面當沒有符合心態過濾的貼文時顯示
**輸出路徑**：`public/illustrations/empty-discover.png`
**尺寸建議**：800 × 600（4:3）或方形 800 × 800
**搭配文案**：「目前沒有符合的貼文。試試發一篇？」

## 概念

一張空的小書桌、上面有一張白紙和一支筆，旁邊一杯茶。意象是「等你寫第一篇」。風格輕盈，不要灰暗（避免感覺像錯誤頁）。

## Prompt

```
A soft watercolor illustration: a small wooden writing desk viewed from a slight 3/4 angle,
on it lies a single blank piece of paper, a fountain pen resting beside it, a tea cup with steam.
The scene is intentionally sparse and inviting — empty but warm.
A few floating petals or paper page-corners drift around the desk softly.
Background is a clean cream parchment wash with subtle paper texture.

soft watercolor illustration, hand-painted texture, slightly grainy paper background,
warm parchment color palette (#F8F1E4 background, sage green #6B8064 and amber #D9B884
as small accent on tea steam and pen,
walnut #3D2F1F for fine outlines), literary cozy mood,
square or 4:3 composition with central focal point and clean negative space around,
gentle bleeding edges, no harsh ink lines, no neon colors,
no figures, no text in image.
```

## Avoid

- 灰色調（不要看起來像 error/empty error 頁）
- 雜亂物品
