# 02 — Onboarding 第 2 頁：以書識人

**頁面文案**：「以書識人」+「用一本書代表你自己。讓對的人從你的書架走向你。」
**用途**：`/intro/2` 全螢幕背景
**輸出路徑**：`public/illustrations/intro-2.png`
**尺寸建議**：1080 × 1920（9:16）

## 概念

一個小人坐在打開的書頁裡，書頁變成書架，書架上每一本書折射出他的個性碎片（一杯咖啡、一片葉子、一輪月、一支筆）。

## Prompt

```
A soft watercolor illustration: a single small hooded figure (round head, two tiny dot eyes, no other facial features,
sage green robe in the style of the Folio logo) sits cross-legged on the open pages of a giant floating book.
The pages of the giant book curl up at the sides and become tall bookshelves on either side,
filled with quirky tiny objects nested between books — a steaming coffee cup, a single leaf, a crescent moon,
a vintage pen, a tiny potted cactus — each one a fragment of a person's identity.
Soft warm light from above. The figure looks up gently at one floating book.

soft watercolor illustration, hand-painted texture, slightly grainy paper background,
warm parchment color palette (#F8F1E4 background, sage green #6B8064 character tone,
lavender #9B8FB5 and amber #D9B884 as small accents on objects, walnut #3D2F1F for fine outlines),
literary cozy mood, vertical 9:16 composition with bottom 35% mostly empty cream space
(reserved for title text overlay), gentle bleeding edges,
no harsh ink lines, no neon colors, single figure only,
no text in image.
```

## Avoid

- 多個小人（這頁是「以書識人 = 個人」）
- 打字機、電腦
- 太密、太忙的構圖（要留下文字呼吸空間）
